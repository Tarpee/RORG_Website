The Royal Guard

"The Royal Guard is the Tank Class of Ragnarok online,
armed with spears and swords and the RG's trademark equipment the shield
they mainly have the highest HP and Defense among RO's Classes, and can serve as good breakers of enemy lines,

high HP means better survivability during WOE or PVP, Spears also have higher range than other weapons and the shield which any good RG will possess will usually be compunded with a card that greatly reduces damage,

the class also possesses some interesting skills that increase Def or even boost the Stats of the class greatly

armed with a strong weapon and a tough shield the RG can serve as an interesting character to play as in RO.
